Voided's Music Discs is a mod that I have decided to make because I am not happy with the amount of good music disc mods for Minecraft, most of them are either old, or don't add many music discs. I noticed a playlist on YouTube called "ALL Minecraft fan-made music discs", and I decided to try to add as many of them as possible.

As of the 1.0 update, I have added 75 music discs, with many more to follow.
