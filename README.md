# Voided's More Music Discs
![version](https://img.shields.io/badge/minecraft_version-1.21-green)
![mod version](https://img.shields.io/badge/version-1.0-blue)
[![licence](https://img.shields.io/badge/licence-MIT-important)](https://github.com/Voided1198/more-music-discs-1.21/blob/master/LICENCE)
[![github](https://img.shields.io/badge/github-repository-blue?logo=github)](https://github.com/Voided1198/more-music-discs-1.21)

Voided's Music Discs is a mod that I have decided to make because I am not happy with the amount of good music disc mods for Minecraft, most of them are either old, or only add a few random seeming music discs. I noticed a playlist on YouTube called "ALL Minecraft fan-made music discs", and I decided to try to add as many of them as possible.

As of the 1.0 update, I have added 75 music discs, with many more to follow.

## Planned Updates

- 1.1: Finish adding all the discs of the people that have helped me (Giving textures and music files, including getting more than one of Lost's discs into the mod, sorry Lost!). Add the newer Minecraft discs to backport them.
- 1.2: Work through more of the list, try to add about 60 more discs.
- ???
- 2.0: Add obtainment methods for the discs, likely through a disc engraver workstation, or something similar.
